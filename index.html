FS Coding

General Info

FS Coding is the student-run programming initiative of Frankfurt School where students can come together to learn/improve their coding skills and practice working on projects in groups. Due to the high variance in preknowledge everyone can fit in well, beginners learn from the advanced members while the advanced programmers are reminded of the beginnings of their programming career. The group is usually split up into two groups:

  • The beginners group. For everyone that is just starting to learn programming we teach the basics of Python in a way that anyone can understand it.
  • At the moment, everyone else has split up into two projects that we work on in the groups.
